Degrees of Consequence

Tanvi Vidyala · Nithya Nair · Viela Lansangam

So far, we have built all of our planned interactions, visualizations, and graphics. For our interactions, we have an interactive globe with a tooltip and rotating knobs to adjust for different policies that will then alter the environment of the story. The story is told through a scrollytelling mechanic that achieves various animations, such as filling in grids and traveling through a line plot. We also designed an outline of our story, which we plan to have change with different knobs and settings.

The most challenging part of our design will be implementing more types of interactive mechanics. Right now, there aren't many ways to interact with the site other than scrolling, which can cause fatigue after a while. To switch things up, we will also implement sliders for our line charts that allow the user to change the year they are viewing. Additionally, we need to work on our globe graphic, as it is difficult to hover over smaller countries. Adding a zoom mechanic to this visualization will likely benefit readability.
